Bio


Hi hello!

I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator born and raised in Oahu, Hawaii. I am currently pursuing my BFA in Graphic Design at Western Washington University (WWU) in Bellingham WA. I enjoy developing brand identities, illustrating, as well as creating 2D motion graphics. Professionally, I work as a designer for my university's primary interdisciplinary academic student journal, within this role I am tasked with the responsibility to design and illustrate these submissions for journals in a way to be accessible to all students and faculty at WWU regardless of their academic background. As someone who was raised in a multicultural household in a melting pot state I enjoy celebrating various cultures within my graphic design, 2D animation and illustration. I like helping people and achieving solutions via purposeful design and meaningful aesthetic choices while keeping accessibility in mind.



My 3 favorite design projects

I enjoy all aspects of design, but I am most drawn to brand idenity design, 2D cell animation design and Illustration!


Folksi

A wool sock brand created through a combination of Ireish & Icelandic wool. Folksi grew into a celebration of two cultures, honoring the land, the sheep and the traditions that inspire every stitch.


Merlin Title Sequence

I aimed to create a cohesively designed and illustrated title sequence which focuses on the motifs found in the show rather than actual scenes. The entire motion piece is done through frame by frame cel animation in Procreate and refined in Adobe After effects.


Glogg Redesign

This project was a packaging and brand redesign for a swedish mulled wine company named "Grandpa Lundquist's"!
